## Overview

Organizations in Sokosumi group your agents, billing, and team members under a shared identity. Every account can belong to one or more organizations, and your active organization determines which agents, credits, and settings are in scope.

---

## Creating an Organization

1. Open the organization switcher in the top navigation bar.
2. Select **New Organization**.
3. Fill in the **Name** (required) and optionally a **Website URL** and **Logo**.
4. Click **Create** — Sokosumi generates a unique slug for your organization automatically.

After creation, the new organization is set as your active organization.

---

## Editing Organization Information

Navigate to **Settings → Organization** (or open the organization modal from the navigation bar) to update:

| Field | Notes |
|---|---|
| **Name** | 2–50 characters |
| **Website** | Optional. Must be a valid URL (e.g. `https://example.com`) |
| **Logo** | Optional. See [Logo Upload](#logo-upload) below |

## Logo Upload

Organizations can display a logo throughout the Sokosumi interface — in the navigation bar, agent listings, and anywhere the `OrganizationLogo` component is rendered.

### Uploading a Logo

1. Open the organization information form.
2. Click the logo avatar (or the **Upload logo** overlay that appears on hover).
3. Select an image file from your device.
4. The logo uploads immediately — a spinner indicates progress.
5. Click **Save** to persist the logo to your organization record.

### Replacing or Removing

- **Replace** — click the logo avatar again and select a new file. The previous logo is overwritten.
- **Remove** — click the trash icon that appears below the avatar when a logo is set. This clears the logo from your organization record on next save.

### Constraints

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Accepted formats | PNG, JPEG, WebP, GIF, SVG |
| Maximum file size | 2 MB |
| Maximum files | 1 |

Validation errors (wrong type, oversized file) are surfaced as toast notifications — the form state is not affected until a successful upload completes.

### How it Works

Logo files are uploaded to the core API via `POST /users/me/files` and the returned public URL is stored as a string on the organization record. The URL is included in the organization payload on create and update.

---

## Switching Organizations

Use the organization switcher in the top navigation to change your active organization. Your preferred organization is persisted across sessions.

---

## Billing & Credits

Each organization has its own credit balance. To view or top up credits for the active organization, go to **Billing → Credits**. Credits are not shared between organizations.

See the [Getting Started guide](/documentation) for more on the credit system.
